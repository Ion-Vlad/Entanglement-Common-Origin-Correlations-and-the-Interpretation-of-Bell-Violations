# Disclaimer

This manuscript presents an interpretive hypothesis concerning the physical meaning of quantum correlations.

The work:

- does **not** claim that Bell's theorem is mathematically incorrect,
- does **not** claim that Bell-test experiments are invalid,
- does **not** propose faster-than-light communication.

Instead, it explores the possibility that the standard quantum correlation function may incorporate a supplementary angular contribution beyond the minimal common-source correlation established during particle creation.

The ideas presented should be regarded as speculative and are offered to stimulate discussion on the foundations of quantum mechanics.
